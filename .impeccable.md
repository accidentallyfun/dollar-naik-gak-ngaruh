## Design Context

### Users
Indonesian general public (18-40), primarily casual social media users who encounter viral political/economic content. They're skeptical of government claims about the economy and want straightforward, jargon-free explanations.

### Brand Personality
Raw, confrontational, honest — "nangis bareng" energy. Like a smart friend explaining things bluntly at a warung kopi.

### Aesthetic Direction
Editorial zine meets Twitter thread. High contrast, bold typography, intentional rawness. Not corporate, not polished — authentic and slightly aggressive. Light theme (daytime reading, coffee break browsing). Reds and warm neutrals. Bricolage Grotesque for display headlines (chunky, characterful), Sora for body (clean but not corporate).

### Design Principles
1. Every element earns its place — no decorative fluff
2. Content is king — typography and layout serve readability first
3. Contrast creates emphasis — bold headlines, quiet body text
4. Side-stripe borders never — use background tints instead
5. Mobile-first thinking — compact accordions, fluid sizing
6. One distinctive moment per section — don't compete for attention
